About

I'm Gabriel Cerioni, a Principal Solutions Architect at Redis. I design and ship real-time systems: AI agents, online feature stores, hybrid search, and the infrastructure that keeps them fast under load.

Most of my work lives at the intersection of Redis and production systems for the largest banks in Latin America, turning ideas into reproducible demos that actually run: agentic assistants with guardrails, fraud feature stores fed by streaming CDC, LLM rate limiting, and cost and sizing tooling.

I care about the boring parts that make a demo real: reproducibility, tight latency tails, and clean data topology. If it cannot be rerun from a script, it is an anecdote.
